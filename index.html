Smart interaction design is the proper way to solve the learning problem in AI

Smart Interaction Design 11 Oct 2023

The article suggests smart interaction design should focus on Artifical Intelligence(AI)'s ability to collect its own data instead of consume exisiting data
via three steps, namely Reception, Interpretation and Learning.
Reception examples include Tesla's semi-self-driving cars and Waymo car, while intrepretation refers to the processing system like GPU for idetifying road conditions during self-driving.
The last step is to learn human experiences via labelling to specify the expected outcomes.
To conclude, the writer comments that AI is not just about creating the most powerful machine learning algorithm but rather focusing on human interaction in between.
